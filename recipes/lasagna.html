Lasagna Bolognese

Description

The Lasagna Bolognese is a really tasty Italian dish, made of green layers of egg pasta, bechamel, bolognese sauce and grated cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Egg pasta layers
  • Bolognese sauce
  • For the bechamel
    • Butter 70g
    • Whole milk
    • Nutmeg
    • Flour 70g
    • Fine salt
  • Grated cheese
